01 — Tentang Kami

A place people
found on their own.

Our customers mostly didn't arrive through ads. They searched, got curious, and fell for the room.

That isn't an accident — it's the shape of a brand built slowly. From bazaars across Jakarta to a quiet corner in Gading Serpong, The Chamber Tea has been shaped detail by detail: the ceramic, the shade-grown leaf, the way the cold brew converts a sceptic by the third sip.

We don't think of it as a café. We think of it as a chamber — a small, considered room where matcha and me-time finally have a place to meet.

02 — Filosofi

Three things we
obsess over.

i

The room before the menu.

Light low, sound softer, ceramic in your hands. The chamber is designed so the drink lands inside an atmosphere — not the other way around.

ii

Leaf, properly chosen.

Stone-ground, shade-grown matcha sourced from small Japanese producers. We change the lineup with the seasons and tell you which harvest you're drinking.

iii

Conversion by sip.

The cold brew is the gateway. We've watched it turn matcha-sceptics into regulars more times than we can count — quietly, without a slogan.
